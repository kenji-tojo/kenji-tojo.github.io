About

I'm a second-year masters student of the Department of Creative Informatics at the University of Tokyo, advised by Prof. Nobuyuki Umetani.

My research interest includes emerging graphics techniques such as inverse/differentiable rendering and neural scene/geometry representations. I'm also interested in applying these analysis-by-synthesis methods in interactive content creation.
